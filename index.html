Bio

I'm a postdoctral researcher at ECE of Wayne State University mentored by Prof. Le Yi Wang. I received my Ph.D. degree from AMSS in 2018 under supervision of Prof. Lei Guo. I spent my undergrad years at Beihang University from 2009 to 2013.

My current research interests include networked systems, distributed adaptive filters, adaptive signal processing, distributed control theory and machine learning.

CV will be provided upon request.
